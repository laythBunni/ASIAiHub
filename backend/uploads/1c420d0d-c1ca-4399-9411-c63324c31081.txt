ASI OS HUMAN RESOURCES POLICY MANUAL

LEAVE MANAGEMENT POLICY

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
- All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of annual leave per year
- Part-time employees receive pro-rata annual leave based on working hours
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance via the leave management system
- Annual leave cannot be carried over to the next year without management approval
- Maximum 5 consecutive days can be taken without senior management approval

2. SICK LEAVE
- Employees are entitled to up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave does not accumulate year to year
- Return to work meetings required after extended sick leave

3. EMERGENCY LEAVE
- Emergency leave can be approved by direct manager for unforeseen circumstances
- Documentation may be required within 48 hours
- Emergency leave is deducted from annual leave balance
- Examples: family emergencies, natural disasters, urgent medical situations

4. MATERNITY/PATERNITY LEAVE
- Eligible employees entitled to statutory maternity/paternity leave
- Additional unpaid leave may be granted at management discretion
- Return to work plans must be discussed 4 weeks before return date

5. LEAVE APPROVAL PROCESS
- Submit leave request via HR portal
- Manager approval required within 5 business days
- HR team notified automatically for tracking purposes
- Calendar integration ensures team coverage planning

For questions regarding leave policies, contact HR at hr@asi-os.com or extension 2150.
