
ASI Travel Policy Test Document

EXPENSE LIMITS:
- Hotels: $200/night domestic, $300/night international
- Meals: $75/day domestic, $100/day international
- Transportation: Reasonable costs with receipts

APPROVAL PROCESS:
1. Submit request 2 weeks in advance
2. Manager approval required
3. Finance review for expenses over $2000

IT SECURITY WHILE TRAVELING:
- Use company VPN at all times
- Avoid public WiFi for work
- Backup data before travel
